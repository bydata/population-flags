World Population

What if we displayed the countries as circle-shaped flags and sized them proportiontally by their population?

Zoom in with the mouse to make countries with small populations visible. Hover over a flag to see the exact population figure.

The cartogram is based on the Observable notebook Population Flags by Ansgar Wolsing

Sources

  • Country geometries: GISCO, Natural Earth.
  • Population: Worldbank (via {gt} R package)
  • Flags: https://github.com/HatScripts/circle-flags
